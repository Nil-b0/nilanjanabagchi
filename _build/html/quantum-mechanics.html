Quantum Mechanics

I explored the numerical solution of the time-independent Schrödinger equation for different potential wells and barrier systems. The aim was to gain physical insight into the quantum behavior of particles under various boundary conditions and force fields.

Key simulations included:

  • Infinite and finite square wells
  • Harmonic oscillator potential
  • Barrier tunneling and reflection probabilities

Implemented these using custom Python solvers with finite difference methods and visualized wavefunctions and energy levels dynamically. These models were later adapted to match conditions relevant to cosmological quantum fields.
