Gamma Ray Bursts

  • Using Python libraries (GaPP, Astropy, Cobaya) for computational astrophysics

We explore two forms of the GRB Luminosity Function (LF):

  • Broken Power-Law (BPL) LF
  • Triple Power-Law (TPL) LF

Three evolution models are considered:

  • No evolution
  • Luminosity evolution
  • Density evolution

Key findings:

  • The no evolution model is statistically ruled out.
  • Both luminosity and density evolution models fit the data well.
  • Unlike LGRBs (which prefer TPL), our Type II sample favors a Broken Power-Law LF.
